# Remote Work Policy

Employees in eligible roles may work remotely up to two days per week without additional approval. Remote work beyond two days in a week requires approval from both the employee's manager and the People Operations team.

Employees must remain available during their team's published core collaboration hours from 10:00 a.m. to 3:00 p.m. local time. During remote work days, employees are expected to respond to internal messages within one business hour unless they are in a meeting, on approved leave, or handling a customer incident.

All remote work must be performed from a private, secure location. Working from public spaces is permitted only when a privacy screen is used and confidential conversations cannot be overheard. Employees may not print confidential customer information in shared workspaces.

Remote employees must use company-managed devices, multifactor authentication, and the corporate VPN when accessing internal systems. Personal devices may not be used to access production systems or customer data unless a written exception is approved by Security.

Employees planning to work outside their home country for more than five business days must obtain written approval from People Operations and Finance at least ten business days in advance. Approval depends on tax, payroll, export control, and data handling constraints.

Managers should review remote work arrangements quarterly to confirm that performance, collaboration, and security requirements continue to be met.
